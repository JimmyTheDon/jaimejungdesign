VITREO.US

Vitreo.Us is the future cultural hub of nutrition, bringing farming, and food production closer to where we live. By merging farming, factory, and dining to reveal the all too often hidden processes of food manufacture and distribution. Vitreo.Us ensures the integrity of the what we eat and allows consumers to engage in researching, prototyping and improving the quality and availability and food that fuels us.

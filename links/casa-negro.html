CASA NEGRO BY ALBERTO KALACH

Casa Negro is the five volumes that make up the house in Contadero, Cuajimalpa, located on a steep sloping heels. It has various structure techniques and materials to explore.

Modeling a section of the house allowed to explore the structure of exposed concrete, steel and glass walls, and the floors of stones and wood.

Team: Jaime Jung, Gloria Kiiza, Klea Bhaja
